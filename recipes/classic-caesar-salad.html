Caesar Salad

Description

Caesar salad is a timeless and refreshing dish made with crisp romaine lettuce, crunchy croutons, and a tangy homemade dressing. Topped with Parmesan cheese, it's perfect as a side or light main.

Ingredients

  • Romaine Lettuce:

    1 large head, chopped.

  • Croutons:

    ½ cup.

  • Parmesan Cheese:

    ¼ cup grated.

  • Dressing Base:

    1 garlic clove, 2 anchovy fillets (optional), 1 tsp Dijon mustard, 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce, 2 tbsp lemon juice, 1 egg yolk (or 1 tbsp mayo), ⅓ cup olive oil, salt & pepper to taste.

Steps

  1. Make Dressing: Whisk garlic, anchovies, mustard, Worcestershire, lemon juice, and egg yolk. Slowly add olive oil and whisk until smooth. Season with salt and pepper.
  2. Assemble Salad: Toss chopped romaine with dressing, croutons, and Parmesan.
  3. Serve: Plate and serve immediately for best texture and taste.

Fresh, tangy, and satisfying—enjoy your Caesar salad any time!
