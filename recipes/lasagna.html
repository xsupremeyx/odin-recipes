Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta sheets, rich meat or vegetable sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese, typically mozzarella or Parmesan. Baked until golden and bubbling, it offers a comforting blend of textures and flavors. Variations of lasagna exist around the world, but its hearty, layered structure remains a defining feature.

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over the basics:

Ingredients

  • Meat:

    Sweet Italian sausage and lean ground beef.

  • Onion and Garlic:

    One onion and two cloves of garlic.

  • Tomato products:

    Can of crushed tomatos, two cans of tomato sauce and two cans of tomato paste.

  • Sugar:

    Two tablespoons of white sugar.

  • Spices and Seasoning:

    Fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds and black pepper.

  • Lasagna noodles:

    store-bought or homemade lasagna noodles.

  • Cheeses:

    Parmesan, mozzarella and ricotta cheese.

  • Egg:

    Helps bind ricotta so it doesn't ooze out of lasagna when you cut into it.

Steps

  1. Prepare Meat Sauce : Cook sweet Italian sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic. Add crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, sugar, and seasonings. Simmer to develop flavor.

  2. Cook Noodles : Boil lasagna noodles(or use no-boil noodles if preferred).

  3. Make Ricotta Mixture : Combine ricotta cheese with an egg and some fresh parsley.

  4. Layer the Lasagna : In a baking dish, layer in this order:

    • Meat Sauce
    • Noodles
    • Ricotta mixture
    • Mozzarella slices
    • More Meat Sauce
    • Parmesan cheese

    Repeat the layers and finish with Parmesan on top.

  5. Bake : Cover with foil and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake another 25 minutes until golden and bubbly.

  6. Rest and Serve : Let it rest for 15 minutes before slicing and serving.

Enjoy your freshly created home mad Lasagna with your Family!
