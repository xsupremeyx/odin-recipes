Garlic Bread

Description

Garlic bread is a classic side dish, typically served warm and toasted with a flavorful butter-garlic spread. Crisp on the outside and soft on the inside, it's perfect with pasta, soup, or enjoyed on its own. It's quick to prepare and absolutely irresistible.

Ingredients

  • Bread:

    Baguette or Italian loaf.

  • Butter:

    ½ cup unsalted butter, softened.

  • Garlic:

    3-4 cloves, minced.

  • Parsley:

    2 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped.

  • Salt:

    A pinch to taste.

  • Optional:

    Grated Parmesan cheese for topping.

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven: Set to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Prepare Bread: Slice loaf in half lengthwise.
  3. Make Garlic Butter: Mix butter, garlic, parsley, and salt. Spread evenly on bread.
  4. Top and Bake: Optional - sprinkle with Parmesan. Bake for 10-12 minutes, then broil for 1-2 minutes until golden.
  5. Serve: Slice and serve warm.

Enjoy this crispy, buttery garlic bread alongside your favorite meal!
